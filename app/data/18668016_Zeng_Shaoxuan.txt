Zeng Shaoxuan (also spelled as Zeng Shao-Xuan, ; ; born August 29, 1981, in Nanjing, Jiangsu) is a Chinese male tennis player. He competed for China at the 2008 Summer Olympics in the men's singles and men's doubles with partner Yu Xinyuan. In the 2009, ATP Shanghai Masters 1000 (as a wildcard entrant), he made the second round.
