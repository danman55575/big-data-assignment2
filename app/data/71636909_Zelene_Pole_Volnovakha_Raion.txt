Zelene Pole (; ) is a village in Volnovakha Raion (district) in Donetsk Oblast of eastern Ukraine, at about west by south from the centre of Donetsk city and at about west from the border of Zaporizhzhia Oblast. It belongs to Velyka Novosilka Hromada, one of the hromadas of Ukraine.
