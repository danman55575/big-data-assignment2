Zackariah Jozef Kotwica (born 12 January 1996) is an English footballer who plays for side Swindon Supermarine, where he plays as a forward. ==Playing career== ===Cheltenham Town=== Kotwica began his career with Cheltenham Town and made his professional debut in a 2–2 draw with Burton Albion on 3 August 2013. Kotwica scored his first professional goal for the club in a 1–1 draw with Mansfield Town on 13 December 2014. Prior to the 2015/16 campaign, he joined Southern League Premier Division club Cirencester Town on loan until January. On 9 December 2015 it was announced that his contract will not be renewed at the end of the season. On 1 January loan was extended till the end of the season. ===Gloucester City=== He later joined Gloucester City on non- contract basis for the 2016/17 season. In October 2017, Kotwica joined Salisbury on a month's loan, and went on to score 5 goals in 5 games. ===Tamworth=== On 24 November 2017, Kotwica signed for Conference North side Tamworth for an undisclosed fee, to fill the void left by the sale of Reece Styche to Darlington. ==Statistics== : Appearances and goals by club, season and competitionLower league loan spells appearances are not included Club Season League FA Cup League Cup OtherOther competitive appearances, including the Football League Trophy and FA Trophy Total Division Apps Goals Apps Goals Apps Goals Apps Goals Apps Goals Cheltenham Town 2013–14 League Two 18 0 1 0 2 0 1 0 22 0 2014–15 17 2 2 0 0 0 0 0 19 2 2015–16 National League 0 0 0 0 — 0 0 0 0 Total 35 2 3 0 2 0 1 0 41 2 Gloucester City (loan) 2013–14 Conference North 5 1 0 0 — 0 0 5 1 Career total 40 3 3 0 2 0 1 0 46 3 ==References== ==External links== * * *Zack Kotwica at Aylesbury United Category:1996 births Category:Living people Category:English footballers Category:Men's association football forwards Category:Cheltenham Town F.C. players Category:Bishop's Cleeve F.C. players Category:Gloucester City A.F.C. players Category:Evesham United F.C. players Category:Cirencester Town F.C. players Category:Salisbury F.C. players Category:Tamworth F.C. players Category:Swindon Supermarine F.C. players Category:English Football League players Category:English people of Polish descent Category:Footballers from Gloucester
