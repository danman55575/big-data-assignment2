Zdenka Podkapová (born 6 August 1977) (also credited as Zdenka, Zdeňka Popová or Zdenka Novotná) is a Czech adult model, actress, and former gymnast. == Career == In 1996, she posed for her first photoshoot, and in 1998 tried contacting Penthouse magazine. Her first pictorials for Penthouse were published in May 1998. She was chosen as Penthouse Pet of the Month. Two years later, she won Penthouse Pet of the Year in 2001.
