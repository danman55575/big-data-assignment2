The Zanzibar Nationalist Party (ZNP) was a nationalist and conservative Arab- dominated political party in Zanzibar. The ZNP, in a coalition with the African-dominated Zanzibar and Pemba People's Party (ZPPP), governed the island from 1961 to 1964.
