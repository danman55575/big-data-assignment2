Zarysly () or Zarislu () is a village de facto in the Shushi Province of the self-proclaimed Republic of Artsakh, de jure in the Shusha District of Azerbaijan. The village had an Azerbaijani-majority population prior to their exodus during the First Nagorno-Karabakh War.
