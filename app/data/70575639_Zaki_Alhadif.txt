thumb|Alhadif Zaki Alhadif (; 1890 – October 27 1938) was a Sephardic Jewish politician in Mandatory Palestine who served as the mayor of Tiberias. Alhadif was elected to power in October 1929. He was one of the signatories to an open letter calling for the 1929 Palestine riots to end.
