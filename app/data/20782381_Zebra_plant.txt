Zebra plant is a common name for several plants and may refer to: *Alocasia zebrina, in the family Araceae *Aphelandra squarrosa, in the family Acanthaceae *Calathea zebrina, in the family Marantaceae *Haworthiopsis attenuata, in the family Asphodelaceae *Haworthiopsis fasciata, in the family Asphodelaceae *Tradescantia zebrina, formerly Zebrina pendula, in the family Commelinaceae ==See also== *Amazonian zebra plant (Aechmea chantinii)
