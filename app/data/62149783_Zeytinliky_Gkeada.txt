Zeytinliköy, also known by its former Greek name Ágioi Theódoroi (), is a village on the island of Imbros in northwestern Turkey. It is part of the Gökçeada District of Çanakkale Province.Köy, Turkey Civil Administration Departments Inventory. Retrieved 1 March 2023. Its population is 179 (2022). == Geography == It is located 3 km west of the only town of the island, Çınarlı (or in Greek Panagia Balomeni), and 10 km west from the central port of the island. It stands at an altitude of approximately 100m nearby Kastri hill. == History and Demographics == The village is one of the oldest settlements of the island of Imbros that is still inhabited. There are 3 Greek Orthodox Churches in the village.Google Maps The village had a total population of 179 in 2022, a notable increase since 2007, when it only had 86 inhabitants.
