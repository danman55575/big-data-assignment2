Zarautz is a railway station in Zarautz, Basque Country, Spain. It is owned by Euskal Trenbide Sarea and operated by Euskotren. It lies on the Bilbao-San Sebastián line. == History == The station opened in 1895 as part of the San Sebastián-Zarautz stretch of the San Sebastián-Elgoibar railway. The line was extended to in 1901. Starting in 2022, the station will be rebuilt together with the neighboring San Pelaio station. As the new station will be in the same location as the old one, a provisional station entered service in June 2022 slightly to the east of the original one. == Services == The station is served by Euskotren Trena line E1.
