It hosts the administration of Zabolottsi rural hromada, one of the hromadas of Ukraine. From 1918 to 1939 the village was in Tarnopol Voivodeship in Poland. It was established in 1494. Until 18 July 2020, Zabolottsi belonged to Brody Raion. The raion was abolished in July 2020 as part of the administrative reform of Ukraine, which reduced the number of raions of Lviv Oblast to seven. The area of Brody Raion was merged into Zolochiv Raion. == References == Category:Villages in Zolochiv Raion, Lviv Oblast
