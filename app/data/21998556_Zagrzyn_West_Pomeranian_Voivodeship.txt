Zagórzyn is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Darłowo, within Sławno County, West Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-western Poland. It lies approximately south-east of Darłowo, west of Sławno, and north-east of the regional capital Szczecin. For the history of the region, see History of Pomerania.
