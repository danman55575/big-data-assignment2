Zacorisca daphnaea is a species of moth of the family Tortricidae. It is found on New Guinea. The wingspan is 22–30 mm. The forewings are bluish white with a fine dark blue line round the base and basal portions of the costa and dorsum. There is a deep coppery-ferruginous apical patch.
