Zanjan and Tarom (electoral district) is the biggest electoral district in the Zanjan Province of Iran. It has a population of 532,111 and elects 2 members of parliament, except that in the first legislative election it had 3 MPs. ==1980== MPs in 1980 from the electorate of Zanjan.
