Zbigniew Zakrzewski (born 26 January 1981) is a Polish retired professional footballer who played as a striker. ==Career== ===Club=== He made his debut as a soccer player in Lech Poznań, when they played in second division during the 2000–01 and 2001–02 seasons. For the season 2001–02, Zakrzewski moved to Obra Kościan where he played until spring round of this season. In 2002, he moved to Warta Poznań, only to join Aluminium Konin a few months later. In 2003, he came back to Lech Poznań, which whom he won the Polish Cup in 2004. Zakrzewski made 96 league appearances for Lech, scoring 27 goals.
