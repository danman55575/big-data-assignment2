Zack Nash (born September 23, 1989) is an American football linebacker who last played for the Arizona Cardinals of the National Football League (NFL). He signed with the Arizona Cardinals as an undrafted free agent. He played college football at Sacramento State. ==College career== He played college football at Sacramento State. After the 2010 season, he was selected to the second team All-America. He was selected to the all-Big Sky Conference first team. ==Professional career== ===Arizona Cardinals=== On April 30, 2012, he signed with the Arizona Cardinals as an Undrafted free agent. On August 24, 2012, he was released.
