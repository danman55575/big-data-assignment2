Zebina L. Raymond (August 23, 1804 – January 5, 1872) was a Massachusetts politician who served as a member of the Massachusetts State Senate and as the 6th and 11th Mayor of Cambridge, Massachusetts. Raymond was born to Asa and Huldah (Rice) Raymond in Shutesbury, Massachusetts on August 23, 1804. Raymond married Rhoda C.
