Zaib un Nisa Awan () is a Pakistani politician who was a Member of the Provincial Assembly of the Punjab, from May 2013 to May 2018. ==Early life and education== She was born on 3 September 1963 in Talagang. She received middle- level education. ==Political career== She was elected to the Provincial Assembly of the Punjab as a candidate of Pakistan Muslim League (N) (PML-N) on a reserved seat for women in 2013 Pakistani general election. She was re- elected to the Provincial Assembly of the Punjab as a candidate of PML-N on a reserved seat for women in 2018 Pakistani general election. ==References== Category:Living people Category:Punjab MPAs 2013–2018 Category:1963 births Category:Pakistan Muslim League (N) MPAs (Punjab)
