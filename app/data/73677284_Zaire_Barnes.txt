Zaire Barnes (born September 3, 1999) is an American football linebacker for the New York Jets of the National Football League (NFL). He played college football at Western Michigan. ==College career== Barnes was ranked as a threestar recruit by 247Sports.com coming out of high school. He committed to Western Michigan on June 18, 2017, over offers from Cincinnati, Bowling Green, and Central Michigan. ==Professional career== Barnes was selected in the sixth round, 184th overall, of the 2023 NFL Draft by the New York Jets.
