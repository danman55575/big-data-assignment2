Zarcero is a district of the Zarcero canton, in the Alajuela province of Costa Rica. == History == thumb|225px|Interior of Iglesia de San Rafael, Zarcero, Costa Rica. Zarcero was granted the title of "ciudad" (city) by a law on 24 July 1918. == Geography == Zarcero has an area of km² and an elevation of metres. It is located in the Cordillera Central (Central Mountain Range) of Costa Rica, 50 kilometers northwest of the provincial capital city of Alajuela and 27 kilometers southeast of Ciudad Quesada. Zarcero, known for its brisk mountain climate, lies in a dairy and agricultural area also noted for the local enterprises that practice organic farming.
