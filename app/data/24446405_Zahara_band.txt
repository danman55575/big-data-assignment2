Zahara were a jazz fusion ensemble who released one album for Antilles Records.[ Allmusic discography] They consisted of several notable members including Reebop Kwaku Baah (percussion), Paul Delph (keyboards), Bryson Graham (drums), Rosko Gee (bass).Personnel The group consisted of musicians from different parts of the world, including the United States, South Africa, and Jamaica. The album blended jazz, world music, and pop influences and received critical acclaim for its innovative sound. Some of the standout tracks on the album include "The Jam Man," "Jungle Fever," and "Star of the Sea."
