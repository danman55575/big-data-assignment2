thumb|ZeratoR at the ZrT TrackMania Cup 2019 Adrien Nougaret (born 1 March 1990), best known under his pseudonym ZeratoR, is a French video game online streamer, event organizer and esports commentator. == Background == ZeratoR has a large following on Twitch and YouTube and regularly hosts esport events in France, notably his annual multi-game LAN event . He is known for his TrackMania competition . It was replaced in 2023 by Ascension, which is held in arenas in multiple French cities and focuses on a different game each year, beginning with Trackmania for the 2023 edition. Since 2016, ZeratoR organizes and livestreams the annual charity project Z Event.
