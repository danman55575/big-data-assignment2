Zaręby-Warchoły is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Andrzejewo, within Ostrów Mazowiecka County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east- central Poland. It lies approximately north-east of Andrzejewo, east of Ostrów Mazowiecka, and north-east of Warsaw.
