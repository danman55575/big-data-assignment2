Zeritis sorhagenii, the scarce gem, is a butterfly in the family Lycaenidae. It is found in Angola, the Democratic Republic of the Congo (from the southern part of the country to Lualaba and Shaba), Zambia (Lake Bangweulu), Zimbabwe (the north-western part of the country and Kazungulu) and Botswana.Afrotropical Butterflies: Lycaenidae - Tribe Aphnaeini (part 3) The habitat consists of understorey grass in open Brachystegia woodland.
