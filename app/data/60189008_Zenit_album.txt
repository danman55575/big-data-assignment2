Zenit (German for: ) is the sixth studio album by Austrian rapper RAF Camora, released on 1 November 2019, through Indipendenza and distributed through Groove Attack. ==Background== Following the release of his fifth studio album Anthrazit and the subsequent re-release Anthrazit RR in 2017, the rapper announced the end of his career, after the release of Palmen aus Plastik 2 and one last solo album. In December 2018, he revealed that his last album will be titled Zenit.
