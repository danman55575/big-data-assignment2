Zameer Ullah Khan is an Indian politician and a member of the 15th and 16th Legislative Assembly of India. He represents the Koil constituency of Uttar Pradesh and is a member of the Samajwadi Party. ==Early life and education== Zameer Ullah Khan was born in Aligarh district. He is educated till eighth grade. ==Political career== Zameer Ullah Khan has been a MLA for two terms.
