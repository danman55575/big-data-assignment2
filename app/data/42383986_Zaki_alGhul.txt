Zaki al-Ghul (Arabic: زكي الغول; born 1926 – 28 April 2019)https://www.iraqnews-in.com/world/4761794/العيسوي-ينقل-تعازي-الملك-بوفاة- الحاج-زكي-الغول was a Palestinian politician based in Jordan. Born in 1926 in Silwan neighbourhood, Jerusalem, he graduated from the Palestinian Institute of Law in 1948 and held a doctorate from John F. Kennedy University in 2006. Since 1999 he served as the titular mayor of East Jerusalem.
