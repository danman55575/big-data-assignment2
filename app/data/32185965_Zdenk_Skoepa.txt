Zdeněk Skořepa (born August 10, 1976 in Duchcov) is a Czech professional ice hockey player. Piráti Chomutov » Profil hráče » Zdeněk Skořepa www.piratichomutov.cz "Zdeněk Skořepa se narodil v Duchcově u Teplic a své první větší hokejové kroky učinil v Litvínově." He was selected by the New Jersey Devils in the 4th round (103rd overall) of the 1994 NHL Entry Draft and played for Kingston Frontenacs, Albany River Rats, Detroit Vipers, 1994–1998, but then returned to the Czech republic to play 1999 onwards for various Czech teams.
