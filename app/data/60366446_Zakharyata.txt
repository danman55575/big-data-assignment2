Zakharyata () is a rural locality (a village) in Vereshchaginsky District, Perm Krai, Russia. The population was 169 as of 2010.ВПН-2010. Численность и размещение населения Пермского края There are 3 streets. == Geography == Zakharyata is located 22 km north of Vereshchagino (the district's administrative centre) by road.
