Zeytinburnu Stadı () is a football stadium, home ground of the Turkish Regional Amateur League team Zeytinburnuspor since 1984, located in Zeytinburnu, Istanbul. The stadium is an all-seater stadium and holds a total capacity of 16,000 people and is uncovered. The stadium briefly housed the Turkish Third League team İstanbulspor A.Ş. in 2004.
