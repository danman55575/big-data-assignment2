Zetea (, Hungarian pronunciation: ) is a commune in the central part of Harghita County, Romania. The commune lies in the Székely Land, an ethno- cultural region in eastern Transylvania. ==Component villages== The commune is composed of six villages: In Romanian In Hungarian Desag Desághátja Poiana Târnavei Küküllőmező Izvoare Ivómezeje Ivó Sub Cetate Zeteváralja Șicasău Sikaszó Zetea Zetelaka ==Geography== Zetea is located north of Odorheiu Secuiesc and west of the county seat, Miercurea Ciuc. The commune lies on the banks of the Târnava Mare river. The Vărșag is a left tributary of this river; it flows into the Târnava Mare near the Poiana Târnavei village. ==Demographics== The commune has an absolute Székely Hungarian majority.
