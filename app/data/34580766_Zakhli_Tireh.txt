Zakhli Tireh (, also Romanized as Zakhlī Tīreh; also known as Mīrzā Raḥīm) is a village in Kuhdasht-e Shomali Rural District, in the Central District of Kuhdasht County, Lorestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 26, in 6 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Kuhdasht County
