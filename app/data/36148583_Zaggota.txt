Zaggota is a small town and rural commune in Sidi Kacem Province of the Rabat- Salé-Kénitra region of Morocco. At the time of the 2004 census, the commune had a total population of 9526 people living in 1456 households. ==References== Category:Populated places in Sidi Kacem Province Category:Rural communes of Rabat-Salé-Kénitra
