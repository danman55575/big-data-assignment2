Zari is a town in the Palghar district of Maharashtra, India. It is located in the Talasari taluka. == Demographics == According to the 2011 census of India, Zari has 806 households. The effective literacy rate (i.e. the literacy rate of population excluding children aged 6 and below) is 49.18%.
