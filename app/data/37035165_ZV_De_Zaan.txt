Zaanse Vereniging De Zaan is a Dutch water polo club from Zaandam. Originally established in 1893 as ZZC Neptunes, in 1987 it merged with Kroosduikers, changing its name to ZZC Alliance. In 1912 ZC Nereus was established and in 1995 it merged with Zaanlandse Water Vrienden, changing its name to ZWV Nereus.
