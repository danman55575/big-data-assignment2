Zavadiv () is a village (selo) in Lviv Raion, Lviv Oblast (province) of Western Ukraine. It belongs to Lviv urban hromada, one of the hromadas of Ukraine. Until 18 July 2020, Zavadiv belonged to Zhovkva Raion. The raion was abolished in July 2020 as part of the administrative reform of Ukraine, which reduced the number of raions of Lviv Oblast to seven.
