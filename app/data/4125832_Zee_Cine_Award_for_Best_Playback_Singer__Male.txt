The Zee Cine Award Best Playback Singer - Male is chosen by the jury of Zee Entertainment Enterprises as part of its annual award ceremony for Hindi films, to recognise a male playback singer. Following its inception in 1998, no ceremony was held in 2009 and 2010, but the ceremony resumed in 2011."Zee Cine Awards 2012 to be held in Macau" (ZeeNews, 23 November 2011) ==Superlatives== Superlative Singer Record Most awards Arijit Singh 5 Most nominations 13 Most nominations without ever winning Shafqat Amanat Ali 4 Most nominations in a single year Arijit Singh 3 (2016, 2018) Most consecutive awards 3 (2014, 2016, 2017)No award was given in 2015.
