Zanandore is a town in Karuzi, Burundi, Africa in the second order administrative district of Nyabikere. Zanandore has a population of 27,867 as of 2022.. The town has an elevation of 1,543 meters above sea level. Zanandore is situated close to the village Butamenwa and the town Nyabikere. ==References== Category:Populated places in Burundi
