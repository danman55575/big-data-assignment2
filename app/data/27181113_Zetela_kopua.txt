Zetela kopua is a species of sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusk in the family Solariellidae. ==Description== ==Distribution== This marine species is endemic to New Zealand and found in the North Bounty Trough at depths between 1186 m and 2427m ==References== * Marshall, B.A. 1999: A revision of the Recent Solariellinae (Gastropoda: Trochoidea) of the New Zealand region.
