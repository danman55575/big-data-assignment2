Zachary Tyler Eisen (born September 23, 1993) is an American retired voice actor. He voiced Aang in Avatar: The Last Airbender, Lucas Nickle in The Ant Bully, Andrew in Nick Jr's Little Bill and Pablo in the American animated television series The Backyardigans. His film roles include Entropy (1999) and Marci X (2003). While living in Connecticut, he did most of his Avatar: The Last Airbender recordings via satellite from there and New York.
