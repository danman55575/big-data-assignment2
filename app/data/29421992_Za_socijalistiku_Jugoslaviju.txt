Za socijalističku Jugoslaviju () was a newspaper published in Moscow, Soviet Union, by Yugoslav Cominformist exiles. The newspaper began appearing around 1949. Za socijalističku Jugoslaviju soon overtook the role as the main organ of the Cominformist emigration from Nova borba (published from Prague).
