Zadunayevsky () is a rural locality (a khutor) in Zarevskoye Rural Settlement of Shovgenovsky District, the Republic of Adygea, Russia. The population was 38 as of 2018. == Geography == Zadunayevsky is located 12 km southwest of Khakurinokhabl (the district's administrative centre) by road.
