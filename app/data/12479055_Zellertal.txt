Zellertal is a municipality in the Donnersbergkreis district, in Rhineland- Palatinate, Germany. The villages of , Harxheim and Niefernheim form Zellertal. Zellertal is in Zellertal region, which in turn is part of the Palatinate wine region and borders on the Rhenish Hesse wine region. ==Economy== The local economy is based on viticulture and tourism related to viticulture.
