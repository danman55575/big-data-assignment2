Alexander "Zander" Robinson (born October 11, 1989) is a professional Canadian football fullback for the Edmonton Eskimos. He was drafted 22nd overall by the Argonauts as a defensive lineman in the 2011 CFL Draft and signed with the team on May 31, 2011.http://www.argonauts.ca/article/argos-sign-four-draft- picks Argos sign four draft picks He played CIS football for the Western Ontario Mustangs. In , Robinson was a member of the 100th Grey Cup champion Argonauts team. In , Robinson was converted from a defensive lineman to his current fullback position. On August 1, 2013, Robinson scored his first CFL touchdown at home against the B.C. Lions. Robinson finished the 2013 season with 117 receiving yards, on 10 catches, with 2 touchdowns.
