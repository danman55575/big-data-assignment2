Zerogone is a monotypic genus of Russian sheet weavers containing the single species, Zerogone submissella. It was first described by K. Y. Eskov & Y. M. Marusik in 1994, and is only found in Russia. ==See also== * List of Linyphiidae species (Q–Z) ==References== Category:Linyphiidae Category:Monotypic Araneomorphae genera Category:Spiders of Russia
