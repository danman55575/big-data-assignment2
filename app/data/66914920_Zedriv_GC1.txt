The Zedriv GC1 () is an electric 5-door city car made by Zedriv. It is essentially the 5-door variant of the Zedriv GC2. ==Overview== thumb|left|Rear view The Zedriv GC1 was shown alongside the GC2 and GX5 at the 2019 Auto Shanghai. It has dimensions of 3710 mm/1675 mm/1535 mm, a wheelbase of 2500 mm, a ground clearance of 120 mm, and a weight of 1130 kg. The Zedriv GC1 has 5 doors and 4 seats and went into production in 2020 costing ¥68,800 to ¥83,800. ===Performance=== The Zedriv GC1 has a range of 211 miles, 74 horsepower, 36.2 kWh battery, FWD, 120 km/h top speed, and a 50km/h acceleration in 4.2 seconds. ==Design== thumb|left|full-size clay design model of the GC1 in Shanghai The design of Zedriv vehicles was done under the lead of their design chief, Lorenz Bittner, in Shanghai. ==See also== *Zedriv GC2 *Zedriv GT3 *Zedriv GX5 ==References== ==External links== *Official website Category:Cars introduced in 2019 Category:Cars of China Category:Hatchbacks Category:Zedriv Category:Production electric cars
