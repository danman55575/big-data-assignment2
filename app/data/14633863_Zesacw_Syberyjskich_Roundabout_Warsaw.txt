right|thumb|300px|Rondo Zesłańców Syberyjskich The Zesłańców Syberyjskich Roundabout (; literally: Siberian Exiles) is a roundabout in western Warsaw's Ochota district, named after Poles who have been exiled to Siberia. The following streets meet at Sybirak Roundabout: * on the east, Jerusalem Avenue (aleje Jerozolimskie); * on the west, Jerusalem Avenue (aleje Jerozolimskie); * on the north, Millennial Primate Avenue (aleja Prymasa Tysiąclecia); * on the south, 1920 Battle of Warsaw Street (ulica Bitwy Warszawskiej 1920 roku).
