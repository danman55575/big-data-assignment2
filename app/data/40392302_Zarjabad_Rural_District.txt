Zarjabad Rural District () is in Firuz District of Kowsar County, Ardabil province, Iran. At the census of 2006, its population was 3,587 in 776 households; there were 3,131 inhabitants in 850 households at the following census of 2011; and in the most recent census of 2016, the population of the rural district was 2,233 in 698 households.
