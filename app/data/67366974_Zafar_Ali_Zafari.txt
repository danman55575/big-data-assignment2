Zafar Ali Zafari (1930 – 2 April 2021) was a Pakistani field hockey player, coach, and military officer in Pakistan Army. He was a member of the Pakistan national hockey team which won the country's first-ever Olympic gold in 1960, but he did not play in the tournament. He retired from military service in 1980 as lieutenant colonel.
