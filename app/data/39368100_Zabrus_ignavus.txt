Zabrus ignavus is a species of ground beetle in the Pterostichinae subfamily that can be found in Bulgaria, France, Greece, Italy, Kosovo, Montenegro, Portugal, Serbia, Slovenia, Spain, Voivodina, on islands such as Balearic, Corsica, Gibraltar, Sardinia, and Sicily, and in Morocco. ==References== Category:Beetles described in 1907 Category:Beetles of North Africa Category:Beetles of Europe Category:Zabrus Category:Taxa named by Ernő Csíki
