Zeng Yi (; born November 6, 1979) is a Chinese singer. He sings in Mandarin Chinese as well as English, and he is a member of the Chinese popular music duo Phoenix Legend along with Yangwei Linghua. ==Biography== Zeng was born in Yiyang, Hunan in China on November 6, 1973. In his early years, he was a worker. When he worked in Guangzhou, he made the acquaintance of Yangwei Linghua. In 2011, he joined the Chinese People's Liberation Army Naval Song and Dance Troupe. ==Personal life== On November 20, 2011, Zeng married Li Na () in Yiyang. ==References== Category:1979 births Category:Living people Category:People from Yiyang Category:Singers from Hunan Category:21st-century Chinese male singers
