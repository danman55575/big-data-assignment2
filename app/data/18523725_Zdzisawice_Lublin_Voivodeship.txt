Zdzisławice is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Dzwola, within Janów Lubelski County, Lublin Voivodeship, in eastern Poland. It lies approximately south of Dzwola, south-east of Janów Lubelski, and south of the regional capital Lublin. The village has a population of 410. ==References== Category:Villages in Janów Lubelski County
