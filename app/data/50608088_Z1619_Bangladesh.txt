The Z1619 or Gahira (Raozan)–Fatikchhari Road is a transportation artery in Bangladesh, which connects N106 National Highway from Gahira (Raozan Upazila|Raozan) with Regional Highway R160 at Fatikchhari Municipality. It is long, and the road is a Zila Road of the Roads and Transport department of Bangladesh. ==Junction list== The entire route is in Chittagong District.
