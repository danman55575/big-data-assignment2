Zapuže pri Kostelu () is a small settlement northwest of Kostel in southern Slovenia. The area is part of the traditional region of Lower Carniola and is now included in the Southeast Slovenia Statistical Region.Kostel municipal site ==Name== The name of the settlement was changed from Zapuže to Zapuže pri Kostelu in 1953.Spremembe naselij 1948–95. 1996.
