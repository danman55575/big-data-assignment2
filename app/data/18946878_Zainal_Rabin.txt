Zainal Rabin (born 23 February 1975) is a Malaysian footballer who played as goalkeeper. A Malaccan born goalkeeper, has been rated as a good shot stopper but never been called up for national duty since he made his debut for his state country back in 1999. A transfer to club side Melaka TMFC made Zainal a well known goalkeeper across the nation and he spent 5 seasons with TMFC as a regular choice among 3 coaches. He signed for Perlis FA in mid-season of 2007/08. In 2009, he transferred to Felda United FC.
