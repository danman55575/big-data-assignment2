Zeel Desai (born 18 February 1999) is an Indian tennis player. Desai has a career-high singles ranking of 549 by the WTA, achieved on 19 September 2022. She also has a career-high WTA doubles ranking of 510, attained on 10 September 2018. Desai has won two singles titles and four doubles titles on the ITF Circuit.
