Zabór Wielki (; ) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Miękinia, within Środa Śląska County, Lower Silesian Voivodeship, in south- western Poland. It lies approximately north-west of Miękinia, north-east of Środa Śląska, and north-west of the regional capital Wrocław. ==References== Category:Villages in Środa Śląska County
