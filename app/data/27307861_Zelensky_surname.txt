Zelensky is a Slavic masculine surname. Its Russian spelling is romanized Zelenski, Zelenskii, Zelenskiy, or Zelensky, and originates from the toponym Zelyonoe (), meaning 'green'. Its feminine counterpart is (Zelenskaya or Zelenskaia). Its Ukrainian spelling is romanized Zelenskyi, Zelensky, Zelenskiy, or Zelenskyy. Its feminine counterpart is (Zelenska).
