Zethalia is a genus of sea snails in the subfamily Umboniinae of the family Trochidae, the top snails, first formally named in 1918. ==Species== There is one extant (still living) species and two extinct species within the genus Zethalia: * †Zethalia coronata Marwick, 1948 * †Zethalia russelli Marwick, 1965 * Zethalia zelandica (Hombron & Jacquinot, 1855) ==References== * Trew, A., 1984.
