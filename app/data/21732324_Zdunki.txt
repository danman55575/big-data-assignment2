Zdunki () is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Ełk, within Ełk County, Warmian-Masurian Voivodeship, in northern Poland. It lies approximately south of Ełk and east of the regional capital Olsztyn. == Local cuisine == Beef and cabbage form the basis of many local dishes, particularly soft-boiled cabbage leaves wrapped around minced beef. Another speciality is breaded cutlets, and for sweet treats, piernik (gingerbread). ==References== Zdunki
