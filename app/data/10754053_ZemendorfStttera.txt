Zemendorf-Stöttera (; ) is a town in the district of Mattersburg in the Austrian state of Burgenland. ==Geography== It consists of two parts, Zemendorf and Stöttera, both of which lie on the River Wulka downstream from Pöttelsdorf and upstream from Antau. ==History== Until 1926, Stöttera was called Stöttern. ==Population== ==References== Category:Cities and towns in Mattersburg District
