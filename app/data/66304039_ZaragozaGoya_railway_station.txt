Zaragoza-Goya is an underground railway station opened on April 3, 2012 in the Spanish city of Zaragoza, Aragon. It serves as the main city centre station for Cercanías Zaragoza lines C-1 from Casetas to Miraflores, and some regional services. It is the only railway station in Zaragoza which interchanges with the Zaragoza tram. ==History== The station opened on April 3, 2012, with regular service beginning the following day. The total building cost was €44.5 million. == Services == thumb|Underground platform Zaragoza-Goya is primarily served by the Cercanías Zaragoza commuter rail line, with a frequency of every 60 minutes.
