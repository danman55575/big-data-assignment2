Zachary Bennett is a Canadian actor and musician, who is known for playing Felix King in Road to Avonlea. He also co-starred in the 2006 TV film Jekyll + Hyde and voiced Coach Clydesdale on Corn & Peg. He also currently voices Zach Varmitech and Gaston Gourmand on the animated show Wild Kratts. In Bakugan, he provided the voice of the character Shun Kazami in the English dub. He was born in London, Ontario. He is the second youngest of four children, and his siblings are fellow actors Garreth Bennett, Mairon Bennett, and Sophie Bennett.
