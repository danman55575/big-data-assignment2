Zarif Zakirovich Baiguskarov (; born 30 June 1967 in Kadyrovo, Kugarchinsky District, Republic of Bashkortostan) is a Russian political figure, deputy of the 7th and 8th State Duma convocations. In 1998 he was appointed as a judge of the Ufa District Court. From 2001 to 2004, he was the chief bailiff of the republic. Since September 2021, he has served as a deputy of the 8th State Duma. He ran with the United Russia to represent Bashkortostan.
