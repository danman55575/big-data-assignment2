Zalipais turneri is a species of minute sea snail, a marine gastropod mollusk in the family Skeneidae.Rosenberg, G. (2012). Zalipais turneri. Accessed through: World Register of Marine Species at http://www.marinespecies.org/aphia.php?p=taxdetails&id;=598345 on 2012-09-01 ==Description== The shell attains a height of 0.75 mm, its diameter 0.6 mm. ==Distribution== This marine species is endemic to New Zealand and occurs on seaweed washings off Stewart island. ==References== * Spencer, H.G.; Marshall, B.A.; Maxwell, P.A.; Grant-Mackie, J.A.; Stilwell, J.D.; Willan, R.C.; Campbell, H.J.; Crampton, J.S.; Henderson, R.A.; Bradshaw, M.A.; Waterhouse, J.B.; Pojeta, J.
