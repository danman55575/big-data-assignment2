Zeyni (, also Romanized as Zeynī and Zīnī; also known as Zani, Zeini Olya, and Zeni) is a village in Baqeran Rural District, in the Central District of Birjand County, South Khorasan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 124, in 38 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Birjand County
