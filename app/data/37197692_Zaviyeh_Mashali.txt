Zaviyeh Mashali (, also Romanized as Zāvīyeh Mash‘alī and Zāveyeh-ye Mash‘alī; also known as Mash‘alī) is a village in Qeblehi Rural District, in the Central District of Dezful County, Khuzestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 836, in 147 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Dezful County
