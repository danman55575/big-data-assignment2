Zellendorf station is a railway station in the municipality of Zellendorf, located in the Teltow-Fläming district in Brandenburg, Germany. ==History== The stop (line-kilometre 75.2), which was closed in 1995, was put back into operation in a different location (line-kilometre 75.8) in December 2013. ==References== Category:Railway stations in Brandenburg Category:Buildings and structures in Teltow-Fläming
