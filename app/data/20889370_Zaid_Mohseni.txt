Zaid Mohseni (born July 1, 1969) who along with his brothers Saad and Jahid Mohseni and his sister Wajma Mohseni established Moby Group, Afghanistan's media company with interests in television, radio, print, web and directories, IT&T;, and retail. Zaid is Director of Moby's technical and legal divisions. Zaid’s leadership of the technology division of Moby Group has been the key to successful utilization of modern technology for Afghan needs. Zaid is also a lawyer and former Partner and Head of the Commercial Department of the Melbourne based law firm, Wilmoth Field Warne. He has substantial experience in corporate and commercial law and legal drafting.
