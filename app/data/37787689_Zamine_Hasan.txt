Zamin-e Hasan (, also Romanized as Zamīn-e Ḩasan; also known as Zemīn-e Ḩasan) is a village in Gafr and Parmon Rural District, Gafr and Parmon District, Bashagard County, Hormozgan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 195, in 53 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Bashagard County
