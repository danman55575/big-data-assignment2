thumb|Zemgale Olympic Center The Zemgale Olympic Center (Latvian: ) is a multi-sports complex located in Jelgava, Latvia. It contains many facilities including an athletics field and football stadium, which serves as the home of FK Jelgava and BK Jelgava.
