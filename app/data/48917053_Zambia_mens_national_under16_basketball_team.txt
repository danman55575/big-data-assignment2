The Zambia national under-16 basketball team is a national basketball team of Zambia, administered by the Zambia Basketball Association (ZBA).FIBA National Federations – Zambia, fiba.com, accessed 18 August 2013. It represents the country in international under-16 (under age 16) basketball competitions.
