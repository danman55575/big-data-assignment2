Zaosinovo () is a rural locality (a village) in Kondratovskoye Rural Settlement, Permsky District, Perm Krai, Russia. The population was 156 as of 2010. == Geography == Zaosinovo is located 17 km west of Perm (the district's administrative centre) by road.
