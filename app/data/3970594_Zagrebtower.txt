thumb|Zagrebtower Zagrebtower is a building in Zagreb, Croatia, located in the neighborhood of Sigečica, on the Radnička Road. It was completed in late 2006. It is an elliptical, 22-story, office tower with adjacent 8-story office building which includes an underground parking garage.
