Zakrucze is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Małogoszcz, within Jędrzejów County, Świętokrzyskie Voivodeship, in south-central Poland. It lies approximately north-east of Małogoszcz, north of Jędrzejów, and west of the regional capital Kielce. The village has a population of 340. ==References== Zakrucze
