Zacharie Tshimanga Wa Tshibangu was a Congolese historian and writer and co- founder of the SOHIZA in 1974. ==Biography== Zacharie Tshimanga was born on 4 October 1941, in Musefu. ==Works== Tshimanga Wa Tshibangu published the following works: ===Books=== ====Essays==== * Histoire du Zaire Editions Ceruki, 1976Reclaiming African History * Enseignement en République du Zaire Editions BASE, 1986 ====Articles==== * Léopold II face à la France au sujet de la création des droits d'entrée dans le bassin conventionnel du Congo: 1890 et 1892.
