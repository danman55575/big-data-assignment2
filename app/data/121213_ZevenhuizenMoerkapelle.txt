Zevenhuizen-Moerkapelle () is a former municipality in the western Netherlands, in the province of South Holland. The municipality was formed on January 1, 1991, through the merger of Moerkapelle and Zevenhuizen. It was initially called Moerhuizen before being renamed to Zevenhuizen-Moerkapelle on February 1, 1992. The municipality joined the new municipality of Zuidplas on January 1, 2010.
