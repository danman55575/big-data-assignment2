Zane-Ray Brodie Holtz (born January 18, 1987) is a Canadian actor and model. He is best known for playing Richie Gecko on the El Rey Network television series From Dusk till Dawn: The Series (2014–2016) and K.O. Kelly on The CW series Riverdale and Katy Keene (2020). ==Career== In 2013, he played Nick in Jodi Arias: Dirty Little Secret, a television movie about the murder of Travis Alexander.
