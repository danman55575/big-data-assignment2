thumb|Zbigniew Wawer (2013) Zbigniew Wawer (17 March 1956 – 12 December 2022) was a Polish historian, specializing in Polish Armed Forces in the West military history in World War II. He was the author of several books, articles, and documentaries. Wawer died on 12 December 2022, at the age of 66. ==Works== *Monte Cassino. Walki 2 Korpusu Polskiego.
